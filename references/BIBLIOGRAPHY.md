## Papers

- **Signatures of mutational processes in human cancer** — Alexandrov LB et al. — 2013 — *Nature*
  - source: [https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC3776390](https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC3776390)
  - _The Nature paper companion to the 2014 thesis. Introduces the 30 COSMIC SBS signatures via NMF on pan-cancer WGS data._

- **Deciphering signatures of mutational processes operative in human cancer** — Alexandrov LB et al. — 2013 — *Cell Reports*
  - source: [https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC3588146](https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC3588146)
  - _The methods paper: hierarchical dirichlet-NMF extraction framework with model-order selection via reproducibility + reconstruction error._

- **The repertoire of mutational signatures in human cancer** — Alexandrov LB et al. — 2020 — *Nature*
  - source: [https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/322859v2.full.pdf](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/322859v2.full.pdf)
  - local: `pdfs\alexandrov_2020_nature_repertoire.pdf`
  - _PCAWG-era update expanding to 49 SBS, 11 DBS, 17 ID signatures. Current COSMIC v3 reference._

- **Mutational processes molding the genomes of 21 breast cancers** — Nik-Zainal S et al. — 2012 — *Cell*
  - source: [https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC3414841](https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC3414841)
  - _First identification of kataegis; early application of signature decomposition to a focused tumor type._

- **Algorithms for non-negative matrix factorization** — Lee DD, Seung HS — 2001 — *NeurIPS*
  - source: [https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2000/file/f9d1152547c0bde01830b7e8bd60024c-Paper.pdf](https://proceedings.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2000/file/f9d1152547c0bde01830b7e8bd60024c-Paper.pdf)
  - local: `pdfs\lee_seung_2001_nmf.pdf`
  - _Original paper deriving the multiplicative updates implemented in src/mutsig/nmf.py. Appendix contains the auxiliary-function monotonicity proof._

- **SVD based initialization: A head start for nonnegative matrix factorization** — Boutsidis C, Gallopoulos E — 2008 — *Pattern Recognition*
  - source: [https://doi.org/10.1016/j.patcog.2007.09.010](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.patcog.2007.09.010)
  - _NNDSVD initialization used in our NMF. Often paywalled; university VPN access may be required. Leaving as landing-page reference._

- **SomaticSignatures: inferring mutational signatures from single-nucleotide variants** — Gehring JS et al. — 2015 — *Bioinformatics*
  - source: [https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC4643622](https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC4643622)
  - _Bioconductor package describing a clean pipeline for SBS extraction from VCF/MAF input. Good comparison reference for our features.py._

- **A practical framework and online tool for mutational signature analyses** — Degasperi A et al. — 2020 — *Nature Cancer*
  - source: [https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC7073440](https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC7073440)
  - _Signal/FitMS framework for signature fitting (assigning known signatures to new samples) vs. extraction. Useful second-stage analysis._

- **Uncovering novel mutational signatures by de novo extraction with SigProfilerExtractor** — Islam SMA et al. — 2022 — *Cell Genomics*
  - source: [https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC9903851](https://europepmc.org/article/PMC/PMC9903851)
  - _Current-generation SigProfilerExtractor, direct comparison target for our from-scratch NMF. Benchmarks against other methods._

- **Hidden Markov models lead to higher-resolution maps of mutation signature activity in cancer** — Lal A et al. — 2021 — *Communications Biology / bioRxiv*
  - source: [https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/392639v1.full.pdf](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/392639v1.full.pdf)
  - local: `pdfs\lal_2021_hmm_higher_resolution.pdf`
  - _HMM-based refinement of signature activity per-position — math hook for stochastic-processes self-study. Ties Projects 8 and 13._

- **Robust discovery of mutational signatures using power posteriors** — Grabowski DA et al. — 2024 — *bioRxiv*
  - source: [https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2024.10.23.619958v1.full.pdf](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2024.10.23.619958v1.full.pdf)
